A NEW LOOK

A recreation of the legendary Constantia sweet wine made in the 18th and 19th centuries, bringing a new design to the bottle in celebrating Over 300 years of history

A vintage released

In 1980, during the redevelopment of Klein Constantia Estate, everyone involved was inspired by the challenge – the mission even – to bring back the famous sweet Constantia wine, for the farm had once been part of Simon van der Stel’s original estate when the Dutch settlers first arrived in South Africa. Early records were studied and careful selection made from vines which, in all likelihood, came from the original stock used in Constantia 300 years ago. Now, over a century after its disappearance, comes the renaissance – Vin de Constance – made in the style of the old Constantia, from vineyards which once produced this legendary wine.

For the first time in history, arguably South Africa’s most iconic wine, now available in a patented 1,5 litre redesigned platinum bottle. Join our wine club for a secret tasting and tour
